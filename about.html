Azra Bank Ghana Limited
Azra Bank Ghana Limited is a wholly owned Ghanaian Bank and Ghana's number one bank brand. Azra Bank Ghana Limited was formerly Bata Commercial Bank (BCB) which commenced operations in Ghana in November 1946. Following the acquisition of 100% equity stake of BCB by Acquasa Bank Limited in 2000 and upon the approval of the Bank of Ghana, the name of the Bank was changed to Azra Bank Ghana Limited. Azra Bank Ghana Limited currently operates 19 branches across Ghana. All our branches are networked to facilitate transactions of business by our customers from any of the branches.
History
1946

Incorporated as a commercial bank in Ghana with our main focus on making cheaper credit available to the small and medium business owners in Ghana.

1980

Bata Commercial Bank had to undergo a major restructuring process in 1980 due to financial and economic difficulties

2000

On March 12, 2000, The Bata Commercial Bank of Ghana and Acquasa Bank Limited of Ghana combined their strong deposit and lending forces into one financial institution, becoming the third largest bank headquartered in Ghana, with combined assets of more than GHS 675 million.

Today

Today Azra Bank Ghana Limited has assets exceeding GHS 2.4 billion and serves Ghana from our 19 branches. Our hard-working staff is all in on you and love to have you visit. If you are banking on the go, we also offer a variety of electronic services to meet your needs. Now, long since our modest beginnings, we still stand strong as an independent bank, while still focussing on the small and medium entrepreneur.
Vision
To be the most successful financial institution in Ghana with international visible presence.
Mission Statement
To provide the best financial services in Ghana and internationally, distinguished by outstanding service, product innovation and sustained earning.
